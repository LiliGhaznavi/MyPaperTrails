The Beginning of My Creative Journey (1)

Hi there! 😊

Let me take you back to my school days. For as long as I can remember, I’ve always loved creating—especially PowerPoint presentations. I found something magical in the process of putting slides together, arranging elements, and bringing ideas to life. And it wasn’t just about the final product; I loved how others would react, saying, "Wow, how did you do that with just PowerPoint?"

Looking back, I realize that I didn’t know at the time that this simple act of creating could lead me to where I am today. The thing is, no one ever really pushed me to dive deeper into this passion. All I heard was, “Focus on your lessons, take good grades, those things don’t matter as much.”

To be honest, school never quite felt like the place where I could really grow. In my country, subjects like music, philosophy, computer science, and graphic design just didn’t have a place. Kids with these talents often felt overlooked, their strengths ignored, and instead, everyone was measured by their grades, not their passions or creativity.

But here’s where it gets interesting—during all those hours of reading textbooks that didn’t excite me, I found myself creating pictures, experimenting with them, and playing around. One day, I opened Photoshop and just started clicking buttons. I had no idea what I was doing, but I could edit photos and create something new. And then, it hit me: “Wow! What a beautiful tool! How amazing is this?” I wanted to learn more, and that was the first spark that ignited my creative journey.

From that moment on, there was no turning back. Photoshop opened a whole new world for me, and I couldn’t wait to see where it would take me next. So, that’s how it all began—a simple love for creating something from nothing, and a curious mind that couldn’t stop exploring.

Thanks for being here with me on this journey! Let’s see where it takes us. ✨
